About

I’m a Maths student at the University of Nottingham and have been into film photography since about 2016. I love travel photography and architectural photography and in the future I plan on focusing more on construction sites and abandoned buildings. I also run the darkroom for the University of Nottingham’s photography society which has allowed me to further explore the analog workflow and get into traditional black and white and colour printing which I’ve found to be a greatly enjoyable process that can lead to some beautiful results.
