About

I’m a Maths student at the University of Nottingham and have been into film photography since about 2016. In 2019 I became the equipment secretary for the photography society at university and ended up running the darkroom which has made me enjoy the analog workflow even more as it gave me the opportunity to print both colour and black and white negatives and try and teach others.
